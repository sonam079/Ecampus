About Us

The E-campus is the WTO E-Learning hub and offers a variety of interactive courses on trade-related matters and multilateral trade agreements. The E-Learning curriculum is part of the WTO technical assistance programme for government officials from developing WTO Members and Observers. Eligible participants who successfully complete the course receive a WTO Certificate. Self-training material is also freely available in the course catalogue to non-eligible learners.
